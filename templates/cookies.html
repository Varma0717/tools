Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience.

Last updated: January 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our platform.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing and performance optimization
  • Basic functionality and navigation

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how users interact with our platform:

  • Page views and user engagement metrics
  • Tool usage patterns and preferences
  • Error tracking and performance monitoring
  • A/B testing and feature optimization

Functional Cookies

Enhance your experience by remembering your choices:

  • Language and region preferences
  • Display settings and customizations
  • Recently used tools and analyses
  • Dashboard layout preferences

Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertisements (with your consent):

  • Tracking conversion from marketing campaigns
  • Personalizing promotional content
  • Retargeting and remarketing efforts
  • Social media integration and sharing

Third-Party Cookies

We work with trusted partners who may set cookies on our behalf:

Google Analytics

  • Website traffic and user behavior analysis
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Demographic and interest reporting

Stripe

  • Secure payment processing
  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Subscription management

Customer Support Tools

  • Live chat functionality
  • Help desk integration
  • Support ticket management

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies or specific types
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for future visits
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

Our Cookie Control

Use our cookie preference center to:

  • Accept or decline non-essential cookies
  • Manage specific cookie categories
  • Update preferences at any time
  • View detailed cookie information

Cookie Preference Center

Manage your cookie preferences and see which cookies are currently active.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience:

  • Essential cookies: Website may not function properly
  • Functional cookies: Loss of personalized features
  • Analytics cookies: Reduced ability to improve our services
  • Marketing cookies: Less relevant promotional content

Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different retention periods:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain until expiration or manual deletion
  • Most cookies expire within 1-2 years
  • You can delete cookies manually at any time

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notifications to registered users
  • Website banners and notifications
  • Updates to this policy page
